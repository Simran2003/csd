Sustainability Goals

Pejas, working on SDG 16, has been constantly iterating the essence of its mission statement "To develop peaceful and inclusive communities for sustainable development, to provide access to justice for all, and to build effective, responsive, and inclusive institutions at all levels".

EVENTS

Anger Management Session

1st May'22

The online webinar on Anger Management took place on 1st May 2022, Saturday from 12:00 PM to 1:30 PM. Dr. Nisha Khanna, a counseling psychologist who has offered counseling to over 30,000 individuals in the last 20 years was called as the speaker for the webinar. Peace can happen only when anger is under control so this was a great event to promote peace. The main objective of this seminar wasto make the students aware of the reasons for their anger and intolerance through games.

Peace Wall

1st April'22 - 6rh April'22

The event started on 1st April 2022, Friday at 1:00 PM and lasted till 6th April 2022. Posters of Peace Wall were shared on the social media handles of PEJAS and this fun activity was given full publicity by each team member of PEJAS to create awareness among the people in the campus of IGDTUW and Ambedkar University. The creative team created a Peace Wall banner to further promote the event where we had a Wall of Peace. Students participated voluntarily and wrote their thoughts on paper.

Dil ki Baat

16th Nov'22

This International Day for Tolerance , PEJAS launched "Dil Ki Baat". A drive to raise awareness about the principles of tolerance which promotes 'respect, acceptance and appreciation of the rich diversity of our world's culture'.
