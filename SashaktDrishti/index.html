Sustainability Goals

Our team focuses on SDG -11 which is sustainable cities and communities which is the foundation of the sustainable development goals. We believes that making the cities safe, inclusive and sustainable is of the utmost importance since the cities are the root of every other world problem. To combat issues like poverty, climate change, gender discrimination, health etc. one must first make our cities sustainable. Doing so will pave the path to success and fulfillment of the other sustainable development goals.

Gifting Smiles
The Center for Sustainable Development (IGDTUW), organized a donation camp. The camp was organized by the Sashakt Drishti to help underprivileged children. The camp took place in the playground of the campus. The collected materials were donated to a nearby NGO Kilkari Rainbow Home near Kashmiri gate.
